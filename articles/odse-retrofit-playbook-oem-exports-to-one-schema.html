From OEM Exports to One Schema in a Week: ODSE Retrofit Playbook

How to insert ODSE into existing ETL without ripping out your current monitoring stack.

Target audience: Data/SCADA Engineers

Most retrofit projects fail when teams treat schema standardization as a platform migration. ODSE is not a platform migration. It is an insertion layer between raw OEM telemetry and downstream analytics.

Where to Insert ODSE

Insert ODSE after ingestion and before aggregation/modeling. Keep your current data lake, dashboards, and alerting tools intact.

raw ingest -> parse -> ODSE transform -> ODSE validate -> existing warehouse/models

Week Plan

Day 1-2: map one OEM export. Day 3: gate with schema validation. Day 4-5: add two OEMs. Day 6-7: produce a single portfolio rollup and regression checks.

Acceptance Criteria

You are done when three conditions are true: one output contract, one validation report, one cross-OEM query path for faults and energy totals.

Common Failure Modes

Per-OEM “temporary exceptions,” unvalidated backfills, and timezone drift are the three most frequent causes of false conclusions after retrofit.
