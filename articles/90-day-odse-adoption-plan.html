Execution Plan

90-Day ODSE Adoption Plan: From First Transform to Production Workflow

A practical rollout sequence for teams that need measurable progress without platform disruption.

Target audience: Cross-functional deployment teams

Days 1-30: Establish Contract

Transform one OEM source, validate outputs, and publish a canonical record sample. Success metric: one stable dataset with repeatable ingestion.

Days 31-60: Expand Coverage

Add remaining OEM feeds, unify fault semantics, and introduce completeness/error dashboards. Success metric: cross-OEM comparability for core KPIs.

Days 61-90: Production Hardening

Add replay controls, regression tests, and governance ownership for transform/version changes. Success metric: production readiness with documented runbooks.

Operating Metrics

Track transform success rate, validation failure rate, completeness by site, and mean detection delay for critical faults.

Decision Gate

At day 90, decide whether to scale analytics and forecasting workflows on top of ODSE contract outputs.
